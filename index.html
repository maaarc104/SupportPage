Call Of Duty Vanguard

1. If I buy a physical version of Call of Duty:Vanguard on PlayStation 4 or Xbox One, will I be able to upgrade to a version on PlayStation 5 or Xbox Series X|S?

The PlayStation 4 Standard Edition will have an option to upgrade to a digital version for PlayStation 5 through the PlayStation Store. Please note: The PlayStation 4 disc must be inserted into the PlayStation 5 to access the digitally upgraded PlayStation 5 version of the game. This upgrade option is not compatible with a discless PlayStation 5. The Xbox One Standard Edition cannot be upgraded. Please note: Physical editions of the game, as well as upgrade options, will not operate on a discless PlayStation 5 or Xbox Series S.

3. Does the Cross-Gen Bundle and Ultimate Edition work across console families as well as console generations (e.g. upgrading from an Xbox One to PlayStation 5 or vice versa)?

No. The Cross-Gen Bundle and Ultimate Edition will only grant a game entitlement within the same console family – from PlayStation 4 to PlayStation 5 or Xbox One to Xbox Series X|S.

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War

1. What comes with the Standard Editions?

The digital Standard Edition features the base game Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War along with the Confrontation Weapons Pack that includes two Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War weapon blueprints, available at launch.

2. What comes with the digital Cross-Gen Bundles?

The digital Cross-Gen Bundle includes the base game Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War for both console generations within the same console family (PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5 or Xbox One and Xbox Series X). The next-gen version of the game will feature higher framerate, hardware-based ray-tracing, shorter load times, and more. Players also will get the Confrontation Weapons Pack that includes two Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War weapon blueprints, available at launch.

3. What comes with the digital Ultimate Edition?

The digital Ultimate Edition includes the base game Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War for both console generations within the same console family (PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5 or Xbox One and Xbox Series X). The next-gen version of the game will feature higher framerate, hardware-based ray-tracing, shorter load times, and more. Players also will get the Confrontation Weapons Pack that includes two Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War weapon blueprints, available at launch.

Call of Duty: Warzone

1. How do I play Warzone?

Warzone can be played in one of two ways: For Call of Duty: Modern Warfare owners: Warzone appears as another game mode option in the Main Menu between Multiplayer and Co-Op. For players who don’t own Call of Duty: Modern Warfare: A version of Modern Warfare that provides access to only the Warzone portion can be downloaded separately from Modern Warfare as a free-to-play experience on the PlayStation Store, Xbox Games Store, or the Battle.net App (PC).

2. On which platforms is Warzone available?

Warzone can be played on the PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC.
